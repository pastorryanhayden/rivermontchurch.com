Visiting Community Baptist

We are honored that you would think about visiting our church and we want you to know that you are our valued guest. This page should give you a pretty good idea of what to expect about our services and answers most of the questions visitors usually have. If there is anything this page doesn’t answer - please don’t hesitate to call us at (631) 258-1563

Our Services

When are your services?

You are always welcome to join us for any one of our services. Here is some basic information about them:

Sunday Service

We have services every Sunday at 11:00 AM . We sing some hymns, pray together, and our pastor brings a message from the Bible. Our services rarely last longer than one hour and fifteen minutes.

Bible Study

We meet every Sunday at 10:00 AM for Bible Study before the morning service. We have classes for all ages and stages of life. Please check out our ministries page for more information about Bible study classes.

Midweek Prayer Meeting

Every Wednesday at 7:00 PM we meet together for a time of fellowship and prayer. Our Wednesday services are more casual. We hear from a missionary (via video or a prayer letter), Pastor brings a very brief message on prayer, we take prayer requests and then we split up into various groups to pray. Childcare is provided.

What About the Kids?

Nursery

Nursery is provided for all of our services. We strive to create a wonderful environment for your littlest ones. Our Nursery is well staffed, and clean. Parents are always welcome.

Children’s Church

During our Sunday Morning service we provide a children’s church for kids from preschool through 6th grade. These kids sit in the “regular” service until right before the message, and then go to their respective children’s church group for an age appropriate message and activities.

Youth Group

We have a youth group for teenagers. They meet together during Sunday School and Wednesday nights and during monthly activities.

Frequently Asked Questions

Below are answers to some of the most common questions people often ask before visiting a church?

Is there a dress code?

While most of the people in our church “dress up” - there is no dress code at the church. We don’t care what you wear, only that you are here.

What is your music like?

Our music is very traditional. It is mostly congregational hymns, choir music, and occasionally solos and duets.

Am I expected to take part in an offering?

No! Our offering is for our members and regular attenders. When the offering is passed please just let it go on by.

Our Location

How do I get to CBC?

From the West: (Nassau county, Western Suffolk, and NYC)

Long Island Expressway (495) east to exit 71. At the stop sign turn left onto Edwards Avenue and head North, proceed about 2 miles and turn right onto Sound ave, proceed about 4 miles. Community Baptist Church is on the right hand side (on the corner of Sound ave, and Church lane).

From the East: (North Fork of Long Island)

Middle Road West toward NYC. Proceed until Middle Road merges with Sound avenue and continue straight. Proceed about 2 miles to Community Baptist Church on the left hand side. (corner of Sound avenue and Church lane)

From the south Fork: (Hamptons, Shinnecock, Montauk)

Sunrise Hwy (27) west toward NYC. Take exit 65 north (Hwy 24,Flanders Rd), proceed North West about 5 miles and turn right on Cross River Drive (105 North), continue on this path about 3 miles until you reach Sound ave. Turn right onto Sound avenue, and follow it about 1 ½ miles. Community Baptist Church is on the right hand side (the corner of Sound avenue and Church Lane).

From the South Shore: (West Hampton, Moriches, Patchogue, Bayport, and points West)

Sunrise Hwy (27) East toward Montauk. Take exit 63 N (Riverhead Rd) and proceed about ¾ mile till it stops at Quogue-Riverhead Rd. Turn left onto Quoge-Riverhead Rd and proceed North about 2 miles to Cross River Drive (105), continue on this path about 3 miles until you reach Sound ave. Turn right onto Sound avenue, and follow it about 1 ½ miles. Community Baptist Church is on the right hand side (the corner of Sound avenue and Church Lane).
